Ed-a-Mamma

Ed-a-Mamma is a company that produces environmentally friendly children's clothing. They use natural fabrics that are good for the planet and children, and they are designed for children to play in and at the same time are comfortable. The summer collection includes nature inspired themes

Mango

Punto Fa, S.L., trading as Mango, is a Spanish clothing design and manufacturing company, founded in Barcelona, Spain, by brothers Isak Andic and Nahman Andic. It designs, manufactures and markets women's and men's clothing and accessories.

Gap

The Gap, Inc., commonly known as Gap Inc. or Gap, is an American worldwide clothing and accessories retailer. Gap was founded in 1969 by Donald Fisher and Doris F. Fisher and is headquartered in San Francisco, California. The company operates four primary divisions: Gap, Banana Republic, Old Navy, and Athleta.
